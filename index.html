About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on developing professionals for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated environments.

IDR connects academic insight with real-world industry practice to equip professionals with practical expertise in digital risk management.

Our Model

Academy

Training bootcamps and programs for students and professionals in cyber risk and digital governance.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing risk models, AI governance frameworks and research-driven solutions for emerging digital threats.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement NIST, ISO 27001 and other cyber risk frameworks.

Community

IDR supports students, early-career professionals and practitioners across sectors including financial services, technology companies and critical infrastructure.
